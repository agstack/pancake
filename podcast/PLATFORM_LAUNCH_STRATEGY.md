# Platform Launch Strategy & Feedback Collection

## Primary Platform: LinkedIn

**Why LinkedIn?**
- Professional agricultural community (farmers, ag tech executives, investors)
- Long-form content support (articles, posts, video)
- B2B focus (enterprises, cooperatives, vendors)
- Strong AgStack/Linux Foundation presence

**Posting Strategy:**
1. **Main Launch Post** (Day 1): Use the LinkedIn post template
2. **Daily Episode Posts** (Days 2-11): Shorter posts with episode highlights
3. **Engagement**: Respond to every comment, ask follow-up questions
4. **LinkedIn Articles**: Republish Episode 0 as a LinkedIn article for SEO

## Metrics to Track

**Engagement Metrics:**
- LinkedIn: Views, likes, comments, shares
- Twitter: Retweets, likes, replies, link clicks
- Reddit: Upvotes, comments, cross-posts
- YouTube: Views, watch time, comments, subscriptions
- GitHub: Stars, forks, issues, discussions

**Feedback Quality:**
- Number of detailed comments (vs. simple likes)
- Technical questions asked
- Feature requests submitted
- Use case suggestions
- Deployment interest expressed

**Conversion Metrics:**
- GitHub stars/forks
- AgStack community sign-ups
- Email newsletter subscriptions
- AgStack Summit registrations
- TAP adapter contributions

---

## Best Practices

1. **Consistency**: Post at same time daily (e.g., 9 AM EST)
2. **Engagement**: Respond to every comment within 24 hours
3. **Cross-promotion**: Link between platforms (e.g., "Watch on YouTube, discuss on Reddit")
4. **Visuals**: Use consistent branding, episode thumbnails, diagrams
5. **Call-to-Action**: Clear feedback requests, multiple channels
6. **Follow-up**: Thank contributors, share feedback summaries
