**What is PANCAKE?**
PANCAKE is an open-source infrastructure project under AgStack (The Linux Foundation) that democratizes agricultural data through vendor-agnostic, AI-powered intelligence. It solves the $1B/year problem of redundant vendor integrations and data fragmentation that plagues the agricultural technology industry.
